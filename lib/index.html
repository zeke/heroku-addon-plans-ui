1. Name Your Plans

Choose some names and monthly prices for your addon. We've suggested some names here but feel free to get creative. Most heroku addons have 3 or 4 plans, but you can have as few or many as you want. You can change all this stuff later, but any change that affects price, features or availability will trigger a change notice to your existing users, and will require 30 days to take effect.
